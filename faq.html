Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add MultiZeno to my server?
Use the Add to Discord button on the homepage, choose your server, and authorize.
Why is the bot not responding?
Check bot permissions for the channel and try again. Ensure the bot is online on the Status page.
How do I see all commands?
Open the Command Center on the homepage or type /help in Discord.
Do you store user data?
We do not store personal data. See the Privacy page for details.
How can I report a bug or request a feature?
Join the support server and open a ticket.
