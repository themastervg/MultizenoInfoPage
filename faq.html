Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite MultiZeno?

Use the Add button on the homepage and select your server.

Why is the bot not responding?

Check permissions in the channel and confirm the bot is online on the Status page.

How do I see all commands?

Use /help or the Command Center on the homepage.

Do you store personal data?

No, see Privacy Policy for details.

How can I report a bug or request a feature?

Join the support server and open a ticket.
